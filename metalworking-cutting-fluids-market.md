<p><p>The metalworking cutting fluids market comprises several types: Neat oil metal cutting fluids offer pure, high-performance lubrication for machining with minimal additives. Emulsion metal cutting fluids blend oil and water, providing cooling and lubrication for various operations. Semi-synthetic metal cutting fluids combine both synthetic and oil elements, delivering enhanced cooling and lubrication properties. Synthetic metal cutting fluids contain no oils, offering superior cooling and low residue, making them suitable for high-speed machining and environmentally sensitive applications.</p></p>
