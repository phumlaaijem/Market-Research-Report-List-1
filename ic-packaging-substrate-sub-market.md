<p><strong>The IC Packaging Substrate (SUB) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Organic Substrate</li><li>Inorganic Substrate</li><li>Composite Substrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IC packaging substrates serve as crucial interfaces in semiconductor devices, enabling electrical connections and providing structural support. The market is categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Organic Substrates** are lightweight and flexible, commonly using materials like epoxy resins, making them cost-effective and suitable for various applications.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Inorganic Substrates** offer superior thermal stability and reliability, often employing ceramics or glass for demanding high-frequency applications.</p><p>3. **Composite Substrates** combine properties from both organic and inorganic materials, providing enhanced performance and versatility for advanced packaging solutions.</p></p>
