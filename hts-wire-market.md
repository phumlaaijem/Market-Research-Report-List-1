<p><p>The HTS wire market is primarily categorized into four types based on the superconducting materials used: YBaCuO, Bi-based, Tl-based, and Hg-based wires. YBaCuO (Yttrium Barium Copper Oxide) wires are known for their high critical temperature and are widely used in power applications. Bi-based wires, particularly Bi-2212 and Bi-2223, offer benefits in magnet applications. Tl-based wires are less common but provide high performance. Hg-based wires, though less prevalent, are noted for their superior superconducting properties at high temperatures, making them suitable for advanced applications.</p></p>
