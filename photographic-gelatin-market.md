<p><p>Photographic gelatin is a high-purity, specialized form of gelatin derived from animal collagen, primarily used in the production of photographic films, papers, and plates. It acts as a binder for light-sensitive silver halide crystals, ensuring their even distribution and adhesion to the film or paper surface. Its unique properties, such as transparency, flexibility, and chemical stability, make it indispensable in traditional photography and imaging applications.</p><p>The Photographic Gelatin Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.1% during the forecast period.
