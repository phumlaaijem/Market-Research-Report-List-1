<p><ul><li>Round Baler</li><li>Square Baler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small baler market primarily consists of two types: round balers and square balers. Round balers produce cylindrical bales, which are easier to handle and transport, making them popular in small to medium-sized farms. They are ideal for dry materials like hay. Square balers, on the other hand, create rectangular bales that stack neatly, allowing for efficient storage and transportation. They are often preferred for larger operations and can handle various types of forage, including grass and straw, enhancing versatility.</p></p>
