<p><p>The Internal Floating Roof Market includes two main types: Metal Disc Inner Floating Roofs and Metal Partition Internal Floating Roofs. Metal Disc Inner Floating Roofs feature a circular design, allowing for effective liquid level fluctuation while minimizing vapor loss, making them ideal for various storage tanks. Metal Partition Internal Floating Roofs incorporate partitions to divide the tank into sections, enhancing stability and reducing sloshing effects. Both types are critical for improving storage efficiency and environmental compliance in the oil and petrochemical industries.</p></p>
