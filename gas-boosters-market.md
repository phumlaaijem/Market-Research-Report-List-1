<p><p>The gas boosters market comprises two primary types: air-driven and electric gas boosters. Air-driven gas boosters utilize compressed air to increase gas pressure, making them suitable for applications where electricity may not be available or safe. They are often used in remote or hazardous environments. In contrast, electric gas boosters rely on electric power to compress gas, offering greater efficiency and control. These are preferred in industrial settings with stable power supply, benefiting sectors such as manufacturing and energy.</p></p>
