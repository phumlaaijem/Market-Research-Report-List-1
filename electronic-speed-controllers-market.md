<p><p>The electronic speed controller (ESC) market is primarily divided into two types: brushed ESCs and brushless ESCs. Brushed ESCs are simpler, cost-effective, and suited for low-power applications, utilizing brushed motors that require regular maintenance. In contrast, brushless ESCs are more efficient, providing higher performance and durability without brushes, making them ideal for high-performance tasks and advanced applications. The growing demand for precision and efficiency in industries like robotics, drones, and electric vehicles drives the expansion of both ESC types.</p></p>
