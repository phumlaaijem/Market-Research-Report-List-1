Softwood Type is derived from coniferous trees, offering a lighter color and lower viscosity, ideal for adhesives and coatings. Mixed Type combines softwood and hardwood sources, balancing properties for versatile applications like inks and varnishes. Hardwood Type, sourced from deciduous trees, provides a darker hue and higher viscosity, suitable for industrial uses such as rubber compounding and sealants. Each type caters to specific industry needs.</p></p>
