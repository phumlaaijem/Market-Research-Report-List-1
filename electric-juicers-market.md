<p><p>The electric juicers market consists of three primary types: centrifugal, masticating, and others. Centrifugal juicers use high-speed blades to shred fruits and vegetables, quickly extracting juice but may introduce air, affecting freshness. Masticating juicers operate at a slower speed, grinding produce to preserve nutrients and enzymes, offering higher juice yield and longevity. Other juicer types include twin-gear and manual models, each catering to specific consumer preferences and juicing needs, expanding the overall market diversity.</p></p>
