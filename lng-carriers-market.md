<p><strong>The LNG Carriers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Moss Type (Spherical Storage Tank)</li><li>Membrane Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LNG carriers are specialized vessels used for transporting liquefied natural gas. The Moss type features independent spherical storage tanks, providing flexibility and safety, as they can withstand pressure fluctuations. This design typically offers better stability in rough seas. In contrast, membrane-type carriers utilize thin, flexible membranes that are an integral part of the ship's hull, maximizing space efficiency and reducing weight. Both types cater to different market needs, balancing safety, capacity, and operational efficiency in LNG transportation.</p></p>
