<p><p>The trimmer potentiometer market is primarily divided into two types: single-turn and multi-turn trimmer potentiometers. Single-turn trimmers allow for adjustment within one complete rotation, making them suitable for applications needing quick calibration and limited range. In contrast, multi-turn trimmers provide a greater adjustment range, typically spanning several turns, offering enhanced precision for fine-tuning circuits. Both types are essential in various electronic applications, including audio devices, signal processing, and consumer electronics, serving different requirements based on sensitivity and usability.</p></p>
