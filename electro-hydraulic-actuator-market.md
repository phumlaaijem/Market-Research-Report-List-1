<p><p>The electro-hydraulic actuator market is divided into two main types: control-type and switch-type electro-hydraulic actuators. Control-type actuators provide precise and variable motion control, making them ideal for applications requiring accurate positioning and responsiveness, such as robotics and automated systems. In contrast, switch-type actuators offer simpler operation, activating or deactivating components through binary control signals, suitable for applications needing straightforward on/off functionality, like industrial machinery and hydraulic systems. Both types cater to diverse industry needs, enhancing automation and efficiency.</p></p>
