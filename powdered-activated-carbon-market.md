<p><p>The Powdered Activated Carbon (PAC) market is segmented by production methods: Physical and Chemical. The Physical Method involves activating carbon through high-temperature steam or gases, creating a porous structure ideal for adsorption. It's cost-effective and widely used in water treatment and air purification. The Chemical Method uses chemical agents like phosphoric acid or zinc chloride to activate carbon, resulting in higher surface area and adsorption capacity, making it suitable for specialized applications like pharmaceuticals and food processing. Both methods cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
