<p><p>Polyoxymethylene (POM), also known as acetal, is a high-performance thermoplastic known for its excellent mechanical properties, low friction, and high dimensional stability. The market is primarily divided into two types: homo-polyoxymethylene and co-polyoxymethylene. Homo-polyoxymethylene is comprised of uniform polymer chains, providing superior rigidity and strength, ideal for mechanical applications. In contrast, co-polyoxymethylene features a varied polymer structure, enhancing its chemical resistance and impact strength, making it suitable for diverse applications like automotive, consumer goods, and industrial components.</p></p>
