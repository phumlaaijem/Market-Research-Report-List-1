<p><p>The natural gas fueling station market consists primarily of two types: CNG (Compressed Natural Gas) fueling stations and LNG (Liquefied Natural Gas) fueling stations. CNG stations utilize high-pressure compressors to store natural gas in a compressed state, suitable for light-duty vehicles and fleets. In contrast, LNG stations cool natural gas to a liquid state for storage and transport, catering to heavy-duty vehicles and long-haul transport. Both types support the transition to cleaner energy sources and reduce emissions in the transportation sector.</p></p>
