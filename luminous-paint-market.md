<p><strong>The Luminous Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluorescent Paint</li><li>Phosphorescent Paint</li><li>Radio Luminescent Paint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Luminous paint encompasses three main types: Fluorescent Paint, which absorbs UV light and emits visible light, creating bright, vibrant colors under UV exposure. Phosphorescent Paint absorbs and stores light energy, then slowly releases it as a glow in the dark, ideal for safety signs and decorative applications. Radio Luminescent Paint uses radioactive materials to emit continuous light without external energy, often used in specialized industrial or military applications. Each type serves distinct purposes based on their light-emitting properties and durability.</p></p>
