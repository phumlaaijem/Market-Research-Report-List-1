<p><ul><li>Air Cooled</li><li>Water Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The process chillers market is primarily divided into two main types: air-cooled and water-cooled chillers. Air-cooled chillers use ambient air for cooling, making them suitable for smaller facilities or areas with water scarcity. They are easier to install and have lower maintenance costs. In contrast, water-cooled chillers utilize water from a cooling tower for heat exchange, offering higher efficiency and performance, ideal for larger operations. Each type serves distinct industrial needs based on efficiency, space, and resource availability.</p></p>
