<p><ul><li>Front-tine</li><li>Rear-tine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary tiller market is divided into front-tine and rear-tine tillers. Front-tine tillers have tines located at the front, making them lightweight and ideal for smaller gardens and tight spaces. These are easier to maneuver but may struggle with tougher soil conditions. Rear-tine tillers feature tines positioned at the back, providing greater stability and power, making them suitable for larger areas and tougher soil. This division caters to varying user needs, from casual gardeners to serious agricultural operations.</p></p>
