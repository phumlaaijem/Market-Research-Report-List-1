<p><strong>The Optical Acrylic Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extruded Acrylic Sheet</li><li>Cast Acrylic Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Acrylic Sheet market comprises two primary types: Extruded Acrylic Sheets and Cast Acrylic Sheets. Extruded sheets are produced by melting acrylic pellets and forcing them through a die, resulting in a cost-effective, uniform product with consistent thickness, ideal for applications like signage and displays. Cast Acrylic Sheets are made by pouring liquid acrylic into molds, offering superior optical clarity, durability, and resistance to chemicals, making them suitable for high-end applications like aquariums and architectural features. Both types cater to diverse industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
