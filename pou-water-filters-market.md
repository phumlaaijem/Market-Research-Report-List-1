<p><p>The Point-of-Use (POU) water filters market encompasses several key technologies designed to enhance water quality. Gravity POU water filters utilize gravity to drive water through filtration media, requiring no electricity. UV POU water filters employ ultraviolet light to effectively eliminate microorganisms, ensuring safe drinking water without chemical additives. Reverse Osmosis (RO) POU water filters use a semi-permeable membrane to remove impurities and contaminants, offering high purification standards. Each type caters to specific consumer needs in improving water safety and taste.</p></p>
