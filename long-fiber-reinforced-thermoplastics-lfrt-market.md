<p><p>Long Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastics (LFRT) are composite materials that incorporate long fibers into thermoplastic matrices, enhancing mechanical properties like strength and stiffness. The LFRT market can be segmented into Polyacrylonitrile (PAN)-based and Pitch-based types. PAN-based LFRTs offer excellent thermal stability and lightweight characteristics, making them ideal for automotive and aerospace applications. In contrast, Pitch-based LFRTs provide superior thermal conductivity and are typically used in high-performance applications such as electronics and industrial components, leveraging their distinctive properties for specialized needs.</p></p>
