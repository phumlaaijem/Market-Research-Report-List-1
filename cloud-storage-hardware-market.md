<p><p>Cloud storage hardware encompasses three primary market types: public, private, and hybrid. Public cloud storage is managed by third-party providers, offering scalability and cost-effectiveness, accessible over the internet. Private cloud storage is dedicated to a single organization, ensuring enhanced security and control over data. Hybrid cloud storage combines both public and private models, allowing organizations to balance flexibility and security according to their needs. Each type caters to different storage requirements and compliance levels, enabling tailored solutions for various business strategies.</p></p>
